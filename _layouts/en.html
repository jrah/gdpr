The General Data Protection Regulation [GDPR] is a new EU directive. All businesses must comply starting May 25th this year. CyanSec offers a thorough GDPR compliance with a reasonable price.

Why decide on compliance with CyanSec

  • Secure data, secure customers

    GDPR applies to all the companies that hold personal and special data of European residents

  • Avoid high penalities

    The penalties for non-compliance are considerable - for large companies up to 4% of its total turnover or €20 million

  • Efficiency is vital

    You don’t need to research and struggle, we provide a thorough policy document with minimal cost

  • Dedicated customer service

    We are an experienced team, and you have a personal contact throughout the process to guide you through any queries
